ShiftScheduler

ShiftScheduler is a robust web application built using the Flask framework, designed to solve complex scheduling problems for businesses operating 24/7. This solution caters to organizations with 10 or more employees, managing diverse roles and responsibilities across five distinct shifts throughout the day. Here are the key features and capabilities of ShiftScheduler:

  • Advanced Scheduling Algorithm: The schedule generator accurately allocates shifts to employees, achieving up to 60% accuracy. The system learns from past schedules to continuously improve its performance.
  • Compliance with Polish Labor Law: ShiftScheduler ensures all schedules adhere to Polish labor regulations, providing peace of mind for employers and employees alike.
  • Employee Preferences and Holidays: The application respects individual employee preferences and holiday schedules, balancing company needs with personal time off.
  • Google Integration: By integrating with Google or-tools, ShiftScheduler offers seamless synchronization and management of schedules, making it easy for employees to stay updated.
  • Full Employee Management: Beyond scheduling, ShiftScheduler is evolving into a complete employee management system. Current features include handling various administrative tasks, and continuous efforts are made to fix bugs and add new improvements, aiming to provide a comprehensive solution.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed with usability in mind, the application provides an intuitive interface for both managers and employees, ensuring smooth interaction and easy navigation.

ShiftScheduler is more than just a schedule generator; it is a full-fledged employee management application, continuously developing to meet the growing needs of modern businesses. Join the journey to revolutionize employee management and scheduling with ShiftScheduler.
